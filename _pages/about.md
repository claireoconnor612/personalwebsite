My name is Claire and I am a third year PhD student in the College of Information at Unviersity of Maryland. I am currently advised by Dr. Stephanie Valencia and Dr. Jonathan Lazar. I am interested in co-designing communication tools with autistic users and exploring how speech-language pathologists (SLPs), caregivers, and autistic individuals can collaborate throughout the design process. Drawing on my experience as an SLP, I consider how end users interact with technology and the feasibility of integrating these designs into everyday practice.
