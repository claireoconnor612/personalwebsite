excerpt: 'Worked with SLP graduate students at University of Southern California to develop a virtual training that teachers adults with cognitive communication disorders how to use Alexa for their activities of daily living (ADLs). Our cirriculum consisted of a curated list of commands divided by activities such as meal preparation, self care/medical needs, and entertainment. As we all navigated this training together, we had found adults to adapt well to Alexa's functions while affording them with more independence when engaging in their ADLs.  '
